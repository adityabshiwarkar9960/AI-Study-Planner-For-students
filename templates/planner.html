How the AI Works — Priority Breakdown

priority = difficulty_weight × urgency_factor × log(days_remaining + 1)

  • Difficulty weight — Easy = 1 · Medium = 2 · Hard = 3
  • Urgency factor — max(1, 30 / days_remaining) — rises sharply as exam nears
  • Even distribution — hours spread across all eligible days (grace day before exam excluded)
  • Auto-balance — daily limit raised automatically when hours can't fit in remaining days
